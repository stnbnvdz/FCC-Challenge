Silicon Valley is an American comedy television series and focuses on five young men who founded a startup company.

About

Silicon Valley is an American comedy television series created by Mike Judge, John Altschuler and Dave Krinsky. The series premiered on April 6, 2014 on HBO, and the fifth season premiered on March 25, 2018.HBO renewed the series for a sixth season in April 2018. In November 2018, it was announced that the sixth season would be delayed, with production beginning in summer 2019, with a projected premiere date of 2020. Read more..
